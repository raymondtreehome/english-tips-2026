字義變身 ‧ 一字多義

Word Transformers
拾
會考最愛考熟字僻意。下列七個字，課本背的不是它真正想考的：
Mean 107 會考
一般義意指 (v.)
變身義刻薄的、吝嗇的 (adj.)

"Don't be so mean to your little brother."

Fine 109 會考
一般義好的 (adj.)
變身義罰款 (n./v.)

"If you don't return the books on time, you will pay a fine."

Sound 112 會考
一般義聲音 (n.)
變身義健康的、熟睡的 (adj.)

"After hiking, they all had a sound sleep."

Last 110 會考
一般義最後的 (adj.)
變身義持續 (v.)

"The heavy rain lasted for three days."

Order 110 會考
一般義點餐 (v.)
變身義順序、秩序 (n.)

"List the names in alphabetical order."

Present 111 會考
一般義禮物 (n.)
變身義出席的 (adj.) / 呈現 (v.)

"Only a few members were present at the meeting."

Still 108 會考
一般義仍然 (adv.)
變身義靜止的、平靜的 (adj.)

"The water was so still that I could see my face in it."
